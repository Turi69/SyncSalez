The problem

You suspect. You can't prove. You lose either way.

Stock goes missing. You suspect the new boy. You can't prove it. You either accuse him and lose a worker, or stay quiet and lose more stock.
How it works

Blind counts. Locked actions. Real evidence.

Step 1

Set up Blind Counts.

Choose your high-value items — drinks, electronics, drugs. The expected count gets hidden from staff.

Step 2

Staff count what's there.

They tap the count screen. They see the product name, not the number. They count the shelf and enter what they see.

Step 3

App flags shortages.

If counted < expected, the app raises an alert in your dashboard with the missing quantity in naira.

Step 4

Review the action log.

See who handled the item last, when, and on which phone. Every refund, delete and price change is logged.
