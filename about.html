About SyncSalez

We built SyncSalez
for shops the
apps forgot.

A point-of-sale and inventory app for the merchant standing behind the counter — not the one behind a laptop.

Origin

A market in Onitsha. Three apps. Three failures.

We walked into a market in Onitsha to talk to shop owners. We watched three different POS apps freeze when the network dropped. We saw shop owners losing sales and losing stock. They didn't need fancier dashboards. They needed an app that worked.

So we built one. Offline-first. Local-first. Trust-first.

Beliefs

Three principles we won't trade.

01

Control beats convenience.

For a Nigerian merchant, knowing what's happening in your shop is worth more than a one-tap shortcut.

02

Offline is the foundation.

Not a bonus feature. Not a fallback mode. The default.

03

Built around real shops.

Pre-loaded with the products Nigerian shops actually sell. Scan a barcode and it's there.
