A cloud-only POS freezes the moment your bars drop to zero. Customers walk away. Sales go unrecorded. Stock numbers drift out of sync. By the time the network returns, you have lost three things at once: revenue, customer trust, and accurate records.

How it works

Sell first. Sync later.

SyncSalez stores every product, customer, sale, debt, and stock movement on the phone itself. The app reads from the device, writes to the device, and prints from the device. The internet is not a dependency. It is a delivery channel.

When the network returns, the app sends everything to the cloud in the background. No spinning wheel. No frozen screen. No lost transactions. You do not press a button. You do not wait. Work continues without interruption.

Engineering

Built for Nigerian conditions.

Other apps claim offline mode. Most of them cache a few screens and crash on the third sale. SyncSalez stores the full business database on the device — and the background sync is engineered for how Tecno and Infinix phones actually behave.

30+ tables

The full database, on the device.

Sales, stock counts, customer records, invoices, receipts, debt tracking. All of it works offline. None of it depends on signal. More than 30 tables covering every part of operations.

HiOS · XOS

Tuned for Tecno and Infinix.

Most offline apps fail because Android kills their sync process to save battery. SyncSalez uses foreground sync workers and reconciliation logic built for the battery quirks of HiOS and XOS — so no record gets stuck on the device.
